OUR WORK

Feeding Street Dogs

Street dogs often face challenges in finding consistent and nutritious food. Feeding them not only supports their well-being but also fosters a sense of compassion in the community.

Be Kind and Patient

Street dogs might be wary or scared initially. Approach them gently and avoid sudden movements. Let them sniff you before offering food, as this helps build trust.

Pick a Safe Feeding Spot

Choose a quiet area away from traffic to reduce stress for the dogs. Ensure the spot is not obstructing public pathways or inconveniencing others. Feed at the same place and time daily, so the dogs become familiar with the routine.
